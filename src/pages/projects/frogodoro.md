# Meet Frogodoro 🐸

Most Pomodoro apps want too much from you. An account. A subscription. A project board. A weekly review. A habit tracker. At some point you're managing your productivity tool instead of actually being productive.

I just wanted a timer.

![Frogodoro](/projects/frogodoro.png)

So I built Frogodoro — a free, minimal Pomodoro timer that does exactly what it says and nothing else.  
No sign-up, no payments, no features you didn't ask for. You open it, you set a timer, you work.

It also plays ambient sounds, reminds you to drink water, and has two frogs in it.  
Because why not — frogs are underrated and the app should be nice to look at, not just functional.
