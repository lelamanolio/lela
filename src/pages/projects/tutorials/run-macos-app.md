# How to open Frogodoro on macOS 🐸

macOS will try to protect you from apps that aren't notarized. Here's how to get past it.

![Warning macOS](/projects/move_to_bin.png)
<br><br>

Open **System Settings → Privacy & Security**. Scroll down and you'll see a message about Frogodoro being blocked. Click **Open Anyway**.

![System Settings view](/projects/open_anyway.png)

You might be asked for your password. Enter it, and the app will launch.  
 After that, Frogodoro opens like any other app.
